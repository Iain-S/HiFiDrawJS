Balanced Active 2.1 Setup

Here we see how you could wire together:

  • a Focusrite Scarlett 2i2
  • a JBL LSR310 subwoofer
  • two JBL LSR305 active bookshelf speakers

It would work equally well with other balanced active monitors (such as the Genelec 8010A), other external soundcards with balanced outputs (e.g. the Steinberg UR22mkII) or other sub-woofers with balanced inputs and outputs (e.g. the M-Audio BX Sub).
